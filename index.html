Edcuation

I completed my Year 11 education at Whitefriars College, where I laid the foundation for my academic journey. Following that, I pursued a career in surveying, embarking on a comprehensive educational path. I obtained a Certificate IV in Surveying, which equipped me with fundamental skills and knowledge in the field. Building upon this, I earned a Diploma of Surveying, honing my expertise in land measurement and spatial data analysis. My educational pursuits reached their zenith with an Advanced Diploma of Surveying, deepening my understanding of geospatial science. Finally, I culminated my education with a Bachelor of Surveying, attaining a comprehensive understanding of surveying principles, technologies, and practices. These educational milestones have prepared me for a rewarding career in the world of surveying, where I can apply my knowledge and skills to contribute to various projects and developments.

Work

My journey in the field of surveying has been both rewarding and fulfilling, shaped by a rich tapestry of experiences that have molded me into the professional I am today. My introduction to this industry was orchestrated by my uncle, a seasoned and licensed surveyor with a thriving practice in the vibrant city of Melbourne. His passion for precision and the art of cartography piqued my curiosity, igniting a spark of interest that would ultimately become my career path. My formal initiation into the world of surveying came as a survey assistant, where I had the privilege of shadowing my uncle for six enlightening months. During this period, I absorbed valuable insights into the nuances of land measurement, mapping, and the meticulous art of boundary delineation. Subsequently, I embarked on a 4.5-year journey with Terrain Consulting Group, where I assumed the role of a survey party leader. In this capacity, I was entrusted with critical cadastral tasks, including boundary re-establishments, set-outs, subdivisions, and feature and level work spanning the diverse landscapes of Victoria. These experiences have honed my skills and solidified my commitment to ensuring accurate land records, playing an integral role in shaping the dynamic geography of this vibrant Australian state.

About

I am a passionate individual with a deep appreciation for the outdoors and a strong connection to my hometown of Melbourne. My hobbies, which include camping, fishing, and exploring, have always revolved around nature. There's nothing quite like the thrill of setting up camp in the wilderness, casting a line into a serene lake, or embarking on an adventurous hike to discover hidden gems in the Australian landscape. Having lived in Melbourne my entire life, I've developed a profound love for this vibrant and diverse city. Its cultural richness and natural beauty have continually inspired my outdoor pursuits. I believe that a connection to one's environment is essential, and Melbourne provides the perfect backdrop for my hobbies. Looking ahead, my goal is to become a licensed surveyor and make significant strides in the industry. I'm determined to contribute to urban development and land management, combining my love for the outdoors with my professional ambitions. Melbourne will always be my base, but I aspire to leave a lasting mark on its evolving landscape through my work as a surveyor.
